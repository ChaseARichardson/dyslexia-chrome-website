Welcome to Dyslexia Reading Assistant

Transform your digital reading experience with our Chrome extension designed specifically for individuals with dyslexia. Activate a suite of accessible tools to customize text appearance, listen to content, reduce distractions, and much more.

Overview

Dyslexia Reading Assistant improves readability and comprehension on any website by offering a wide range of customizable features. Whether you need dyslexia-friendly fonts, text-to-speech, or interactive reading aids, our extension is built to adapt to your unique needs and create a more inclusive online environment.
